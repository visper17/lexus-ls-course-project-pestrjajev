Maintenance Schedule

Regular maintenance is essential to keep your Lexus LS running smoothly and reliably.

Below is a typical maintenance schedule for the LS:

  • Oil change: Every 10,000 km
  • Tire rotation: Every 10,000 km
  • Brake inspection: Every 20,000 km
Service Code: LS-OIL-10K

Check your user manual for the most up-to-date recommendations.

Always use genuine Lexus parts for replacements.

Engine and Performance

The LS offers both V6 and V8 engine options, including a hybrid powertrain for maximum fuel efficiency.

Performance specs:

  • Horsepower: Up to 416 HP
  • Torque: Up to 442 lb-ft
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.6 seconds (LS 500)
Engine Model: 2UR-GSE

Ensure regular engine diagnostics for optimal performance.

Refer to the onboard computer for maintenance alerts.

Troubleshooting Tips

Common issues and troubleshooting steps for your Lexus LS:

  • Engine won’t start: Check battery and ignition system.
  • Warning lights: Refer to the owner’s manual or onboard diagnostics.
  • Navigation malfunction: Restart infotainment system.
Contact Lexus Support: 1-800-25-LEXUS

Always consult a certified Lexus technician for complex issues.

Keep records of all maintenance and repairs performed.
